Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to all your questions regarding the festival and registration.

1. How do I register for Utsav 2024? +

To register, visit the official Utsav 2024 website and follow the instructions on the registration page. Make sure to fill out all the required information and submit the form before the deadline.

2. What is the last date to register? +

The last date to register for Utsav 2024 is 10th November, 2024. Please ensure that all your documents are ready and submitted by this date.

3. Is there a registration fee? +

Yes, there is a registration fee of Rs. 3000/- per participant. Payment can be made online through the official website.

4. What events will be held during the festival? +

The festival will feature events in categories such as music, dance, theatre, literature, and fine arts. The festival will be conducted from 26th-30th November 2024. Detailed schedules are available on the Utsav 2024 website.

5. Is there online registration for events? +

Yes, you need to get registered online in order to participate in the events of Gour Gourav Utsav. You can register for different events from here once registration starts.
